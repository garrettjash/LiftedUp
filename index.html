The Crucifixion
of Jesus Christ

There are many references to the saving power of the Crucifixion in the scriptures, as well as teachings from living prophets and apostles. This website is a compilation of these references and is meant to bring us closer to Jesus Christ and have more appreciation of His sacrifice for us.

Each page will have references from each of these sources:

The Old Testament

From the time of Adam to a few hundred years before the coming of Christ, many prophets bore witness of His coming and His sacrifice.

The New Testament

The life of Jesus Christ, as recorded in the four gospels, as well as the early church established by Him. His disciples preach of Him and His saving power through the cross.

The Book of Mormon

An account of Jesus Christ's visit and teachings to the people of ancient America, offering a unique perspective on His ministry and message.

Doctrine & Covenants and The Pearl of Great Price

The Doctrine and Covenants is a collection of revelations given through the prophet Joseph Smith, and the Pearl of Great Price is a compilation of scriptural texts, as well as writings from Joseph Smith.

October 2022 General Conference

General Conference is a biannual meeting of members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints where leaders of the Church share teachings and guidance on various topics.
